HOW TO PLAY
  1. Read the clues

  2. Find all the "basic" clues and mark them. The most basic clue is the one that states that something is in a determined house/position. Example:

    • The German lives in house #3.

  3. Sometimes you are going to need to deduce some information using two or more clues.

  4. To submit your puzzle, catch the moving picture by clicking on it.

  5. You will have 5 attempts to work through all the puzzles. Puzzles will vary in difficulty.
